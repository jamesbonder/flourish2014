The Show

The Flourish 2014 live show features examples of some of the very best work from across our portfolio of creative undergraduate courses, including Animation, Art, Design, Drama, Fashion, Film, Media, Music, Photography, Visual Effects and Motion Graphics

The show launches with an exclusive private viewing for students, parents, staff and industry on Friday 30th May.

Flourish 2014 is open to the public from Sunday 1st June and will run until Wednesday 5th June.

The show takes place at our state-of-the-art ATRiuM Campus in central Cardiff. You can watch a live feed of the launch night below.

Our other events

Platfform is the end-of-year fashion showcase and awards ceremony for our Fashion Design degree course. The event took place at The National Museum Cardiff for the third year running and featured a catwalk show and interactive exhibits.

The Glammies is our annual end-of-year animation awards ceremony. The event featured screenings of student work from a number of courses including Animation, Game Art, Visual Effects and Motion Graphics.

The Show

The Curious 2013 live show featured examples of some of the very best work from across our portfolio of creative undergraduate courses, including Animation, Design, Fashion, Film, Media, Drama and Music.

The show launched with an exclusive private viewing for students, parents, staff and industry on Friday 31st May.

Curious 2013 opened to the public on Saturday 1st June and ran until Wednesday 5th June.

The show took place at our state-of-the-art ATRiuM Campus in central Cardiff.

Our other events

Platfform is the end-of-year fashion showcase and awards ceremony for our Divison of Fashion and Retail Design. The event took place at The National Museum Cardiff for the second year running and featured a catwalk show and interactive exhibits.

The Glammies is our annual end-of-year animation awards ceremony. The event featured screenings of student work from across our Division of Animation and Visual Effects.

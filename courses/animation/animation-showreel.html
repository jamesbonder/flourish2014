Visual Effects Showreel 2013

BA (Hons) Visual Effects

This showreel offers a compilation of final year student work from the BA (Hons) Visual Effects and Motion Graphics.

Visual effects and motion graphics has exploded over the past decade. From film and TV title sequences to viral graphics and digital visual effects work, there are now many diverse employment opportunities for budding designers and film makers to display their creative talents.

This award will help bring your creative ideas to life using the latest digital technologies. This hands-on approach means you will receive specialist training in high-definition cameras and green screen technology. Plus, we have successful links with many local and national design and visual effects companies, who can give you extra insight into the industry.

Graduates of this course now work for high-profile TV and post-production companies throughout the country, including The Moving Picture Company, Framestore and Double Negative.

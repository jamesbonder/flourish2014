About the 5 Days of Theatre

The University of South Wales annual 5 Days of Theatre Festival took place at the ATRiuM campus in Cardiff from 18th to the 22nd March 2013. The festival provides final year BA (Hons) Theatre and Drama students with a platform for their final assessed performances. The shows attract sizeable audiences from both inside and outside the university.

BA (Hons) Fashion Design & Fashion Promotion

About Platfform 13

On Monday 20th and Tuesday 21st May 2013, the National Museum in Cardiff was transformed into a catwalk by final year fashion students.

The students on the BA (Hons) Fashion Design and BA (Hons) Fashion Promotion courses at the Faculty of Creative and Cultural Industries, Cardiff Campus, University of South Wales, showcased cutting-edge designs in the historic building as part of the end of year show. The event featured work from final year students with help from Lighting Design and Live Event Technology students who provided their technical expertise in sound and lighting for the event.

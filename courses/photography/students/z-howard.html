About Zoe's Work

I am a final year Photographic Art student, studying in Caerleon, Newport. My work mostly combines sculpture and photography and pushes the boundaries between 2D and 3D. This fascination started when I was in year 10. My current project asks the viewer to consider the multiple ways of viewing a space. I made a four-sided mirror cube that created visual games within the photographs. The images talk of formalist concerns, architectural space and the way we inhabit indoor and outdoor.

The fabrication of my work is an incredibly important element as the folding, then re-photographing of my image leads to compressing the 3D into a 2D photograph. Adding these images to the walls of City Campus brings the institutional space into account.

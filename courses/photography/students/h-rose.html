Harry Rose

BA (Hons) Photographic Art


About Harry's Work

I am in my final year of study at University of South Wales, Caerleon Campus. I work with landscapes, specifically those of north wales. Working with memory and the concept of permanence through natural minerals.

The projects title 'In the company of an Invisible Man' reflects the passing and carved moments of my late father into the landscape, where his ashes lay. Working with old family photographs and my own, to create a dialogue between father and son. Re connecting with the landscape which bares so much importance. I also co edit and founded Darwin Magazine, a contemporary photography magazine.

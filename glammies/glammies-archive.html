Glammies - 2010


Some of the UK’s best up-and-coming young animators celebrated their success at the ‘Glammies 2010’.Held annually, the ‘Glammies’ is held at a Cardiff cinema and enables students to showcase, to employers from within the industry, the high standard of animated work they produce during their studies.
